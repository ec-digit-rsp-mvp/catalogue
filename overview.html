Our mission

« Not reinventing the wheel » probably best describes the idea behind the Reusable solutions. They are designed to help you in building, maintaining, upgrading your own digital solutions that often need the same capabilities. They will help you focus on your digital solutions that can be created faster, at lower cost and implementing standards.

Some of these ready-to-use common services, and supporting frameworks, are based on ‘building blocks’ from EU Programmes.

There are many things to love about reusing
these solutions. You don’t have to:

  • Spend time and resources in building what already exists
  • Host and maintain a solution yourself
  • Ensure technical support
  • Worry about compliance with evolving standards and legislation
  • Handle the evolution of the products and services
The Reusable Solutions Platform will be a portfolio of high-quality Reusable Solutions: managed services enabling basic technical capabilities, such as authentication and identity management, search, document management, electronic signatures, data services etc.

The European Commission Digital Strategy

What are the
benefits

The benefits of reusing are numerous. Research shows that it brings 40-50% performance gains, 20-40% cost savings and improves the user experience at all levels.

  • Saving time

    Time to go-live is reduced as custom developments are reduced.

  • Saving money

    Optimised use of resources by centralising the provision of ‘generic’ functions.

  • Improving the User Experience

    More consistent and user-friendly, with positive impact on end-users e.g. same login for all applications

  • Complying with EU Regulations

    Such as eIDAS and GDPR.
