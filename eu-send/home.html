EU Send Send and receive
files in a secure way

EU Send is a service for exchanging data and documents in a secure way, between EU Institutions and any private or public entities in the Member States and third countries.

EU Send is a managed service hosted and operated centrally by DIGIT or use it as building block

How EU Send can
help you

Exchange any format
  • Send any file format
  • Send a bundle of files at once keeping your folder structure
  • Up to 5 GB* per transmission

*Up to 500 MB per file and 5 GB per transmission for the web solution (eTrustEx Web) and up to 2GB per transmission for machine-to-machine (eDelivery) connection

Exchange securely
  • Sensitive non-classified level of confidentiality
  • Possibility to encrypt your messages
  • Using the Secure Hosting Service of the EC Data Centre

Compliant with the EU Regulation (2018/1725) on Data Protection and Commission Decision (2017/46) on security of IT systems in the EC

Exchange with evidence

How to start with EU Send

Step #01
Contact us

Are you an European Institution or Agency interested in exchanging information between other European Institutions, then contact us to get started

Step #02
Provide information about your project

After you have contacted us with your interest for EU Send, we will get back to you with a set of questions in order to find out which solution would be more appropriate for your specific project needs.

You will be asked to provide the following information:

  • What is your project about?
  • What is the business context: who sends what to whom?
  • Does it concern human interaction / System to system / User to system?
  • Is Encryption/signature needed?
  • What is the volume of individual files/per transmission?
  • What is the frequency of transmissions?

Step #03
Attend a meeting with the EU Send team

Once we have the basic information regarding your project needs, we will organise a meeting with you in which:

  • The representatives of your DG/Agency will present in detail your project’s business case
  • We will present EU Send in detail to you, tailored to your needs as communicated via the preceding questionnaire. You can find the full Service Offering of EU Send. You can understand the differences between eDelivery and eTrustEx.
  • We will give you a demo of the solution (when appropriate).
  • Read more about the Demo Service.

After this meeting, the following conclusion will be drawn:

  • Do you think that EU Send is fit for purpose for your project?
  • Do you want to test the proposed solution?
  • Do you need more information about EU Send from us?
  • Do we need more information about your project?
  • Is there another meeting needed?

What to expect after the meeting
Following this first meeting with the EU Send team, you might:

  • Want to test the proposed solution → we will create test accounts for your team
  • Be interested in the other solutions offered by EU Send
    → a new workshop will be organised focused on other possible solutions
  • Want to have another meeting / ask for more information
    → we will plan a follow-up meeting/provide you with answers
  • Conclude that you are not interested in using EU Send
    → we will thank you for your interest and consideration of EU Send and learn from this experience

Step #04
Commit to use EU Send and let us design, deploy and operate your EU Send solution

When you are convinced about the effectiveness of the proposed EU Send solution for your project’s needs, the EU Send team will start designing, deploying and operating it for you.

Design

Once you committed to use the proposed solution of EU Send, we will:

  • Decide on the overall architecture
  • Write the SDD (Service Design Document)
  • Write the MoU (Memorandum of Understanding)
  • Write the configuration document

Deployment

In order to ensure the affective deployment of your EU Send solution, the following actions will taken by us:

  • In case an eTrustEx component is used, EU Send team will create the ICAs (Interchange agreements), needed to be put in place between two Parties in order to exchange messages
  • In order to configure the Parties, EU Send will ask you to provide the names of the Admin and Operators (and the ECAS credentials)
  • If encryption is used, we would need the Public Key in order to set up the ICAs. (The Public Key might be provided as part of the eDelivery PKI service)
  • EU Send team will setup the ACC environment for testing purposes
  • If you are satisfied with the ACC environment, then the EU Send team will set up the PROD environment

Operation and Maintenance

  • Once the PROD environment is setup, we will include the representatives of your DG/Agency on the Operation Management Board (OMB) distribution list and give you access to Confluence. This way you will have an active say in the operation management of EU Send
  • The representatives of your DG/Agency will be invited to the regular OMB meetings
  • You will be granted access to the EU Send user community and the EU Send Knowledge base. Read more about there services here.
  • We will ensure effective System Operation
  • We will stand in for the Maintenance of your EU Send solution
